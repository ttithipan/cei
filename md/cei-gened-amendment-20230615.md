# CEI Curriculum Amendment — GenEd Faculty Requirement Cancelled (2023)

> 📄 [View original PDF](documents/cei-gened-amendment-20230615.pdf) — source of truth  
> 📅 Dated: 21 June 2023 (B.E. 2566) — Faculty Academic Committee Resolution No. 4/2566  
> ⚠️ This is an unofficial English translation. The original document is in Thai. OCR text is included below for reference.

---

## Summary

The Faculty of Engineering Academic Committee resolved that **CEI students may now enroll in ANY course from the General Education (GenEd) category** to satisfy the faculty-mandated course requirement, effective for the **2022 curriculum (B.E. 2565)** version and onwards.

### What Changed

| Before | After |
|--------|-------|
| Students had to take specific GenEd courses from a faculty-mandated list | Students can pick **any** GenEd course from the updated 2025 catalog |

### Who This Affects

Only the **Computer Engineering International Program (CEI)** — B.Eng. in Computer Engineering (International Program), curriculum version B.E. 2565 (2022).

Other programs in the same resolution (Robotics & AI, Electrical/Communications, Mechanical, Software Engineering) had separate unrelated amendments (prerequisite fixes, elective additions).

### Key Detail

Section 3.1.2 of the curriculum structure, General Education category, is amended with the following footnote:

> "Students may enroll in any course from the General Education category (revised B.E. 2565) in place of the Faculty Course Requirement courses."

### Effective Date

Approved by the Faculty Academic Committee on **21 June 2023** (meeting 4/2566) and forwarded to the Institute Academic Council for final approval.
