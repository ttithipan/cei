## Course Description

Study time value of money, investment planning principles, types, return and risk of each asset class, finance theories related to investment, risk management, financial instrument analysis, fundamental of valuation, and portfolio management.

---

## Course Learning Outcomes (CLO)

1. Students understand and become familiar with various asset classes including stocks, bonds, gold, ETFs, and global equity mutual funds, and understand the risks associated with each asset class.
2. Students can apply investment planning for themselves in real life.
3. Students can search for information on economics, finance, investing in stocks, mutual funds, and alternative assets.
4. Students can choose to invest in stocks or mutual funds around the world to suit themselves to get a return worth the risk.
5. Students have a good mindset for investing — don't stress about short-term fluctuations and can correct errors.
